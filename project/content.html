‚≠ê Description of the Water Flow System (for your video)

When rainfall begins, the circular Smart Rain Collector captures the falling water and filters out debris using a built-in mesh layer. From the top disk, water flows downward into the central funnel, where its volume is measured by a simple float sensor. Gravity then guides the water into underground channels that spread across the farmland. As the water moves, soil moisture sensors monitor absorption levels and send the data to the mobile app. If the soil becomes fully hydrated, the pathway automatically re-routes excess water into a small storage pit for later use. This creates a continuous natural cycle ‚Äî **from rain ‚Üí collector ‚Üí funnel ‚Üí smart paths ‚Üí crop roots** ‚Äî without needing pumps or electricity.
